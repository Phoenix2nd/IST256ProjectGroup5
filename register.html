Registration FAQs

How will I receive my confirmation?
A confirmation email will be sent to the address you register with within 24 hours of submission.
Can I update my details after registering?
Yes — log in to the My Profile page to make changes at any time before July 10, 2026.
What is the cancellation policy?
Full refunds are available up to June 1, 2026. After that date, registrations are non-refundable but transferable. Read the full policy.
Is there a group registration option?
Groups of 5 or more can contact us at register@conference.com for bulk registration assistance.
What accessibility support is available?
We provide live captioning, quiet rooms, mobility access, and dietary accommodations. Use the Accessibility Needs field above, or email our accessibility team.
